Orphanage Information:

Casa Hogar Emmanuel is an orphanage in Naco , Sonora, Mexico established to provide a safe haven for orphaned, abused and abandoned children. Here they find refuge and shelter, love and respect, education and spiritual guidance, and an opportunity to appreciate who they are, and all they are worth. Casa Hogar Emmanuel is a home with peace and promise for the children of Mexico. Naco is located on the Mexican-US border southeast of Tucson, Arizona. Currently, 20 children call Casa Hogar home.
